# Privacy Policy (Discord)

## What Data We Collect
Data is only collected for users who use are subscribed to WOTD via the Discord bot. If you aren't subscribed or have unsubscribed, Word of the Day will store no data about you. Here is a complete list of all the data we collect:

- The user ID or server ID + channel ID of the subscribed user/server
- The selected time zone and time(s) for the Word of the Day to be sent
- Various configuration settings for formatting the Word of the Day message
- If `/config` has been used within the past hour, the subscribe user/channel's icon and name will be temporarily stored to be displayed on the configuration site

We do not collect sensitive personal information, such as real names, email addresses, or payment info.

## How We Use Your Data
We use these data for sending the Word of the Day with your preferred configuration settings. We will not share or sell your data.

## How Long We Keep Your Data
We keep your data for until you use `/unsubscribe` or `/forget_me`. Your data will be automatically forgotten if you restrict access from the Discord bot to send messages to the DM/channel (e.g., removing message permissions, deleting your account/channel, or blocking/kicking the bot).

## User Rights
You have the right to delete or request your data. By using `/forget_me` or `/unsubscribe`, all your data will be forgotten automatically. By using `/request_data`, the Discord bot will send you a zip file of all the data collected about you as part of your subscription.
