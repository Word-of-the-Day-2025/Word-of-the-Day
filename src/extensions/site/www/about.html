About Word of the Day

What is Word of the Day about?

Word of the Day is a free and open-source service that posts an interesting and uncommon word every day at midnight UTC. The word is accompanied by its pronunciation, definition, and part of speech, provided by Wiktionary. You can subscribe to Word of the Day via Discord, or use the free API.

How Can I Support Word of the Day?

There is no way to donate because I don't need money. Go donate to the Wikimedia Foundation instead, they provide word information on Wiktionary. However, you may contribute to the project on GitHub by reporting bugs, suggesting features, or submitting pull requests.
