About {{ wotd }}

What is {{ wotd }}?

{{ wotd }} is a free and open-source service that posts an interesting and uncommon word every day at midnight UTC. The word is accompanied by its pronunciation, definition, and part of speech, provided by Wiktionary. You can subscribe to {{ wotd }} via Discord, or use the free API.

How Can I Support {{ wotd }}?

There is no way to support us financially because we don't need money. Go donate to the Wikimedia Foundation instead, they provide word information on Wiktionary. However, you may contribute to the project on GitHub by reporting bugs, suggesting features, or submitting pull requests.
